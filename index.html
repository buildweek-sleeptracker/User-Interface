A sleep and mood tracker web app

How much sleep do you need to optimize your life?

We all sleep, but few of us feel rested. Sleepmood enables you to analyze and repair your sleeping habits, based purely off of how you feel.

The Sleepmood Three Step Process

First Step

Tell Sleepmood when you are going to bed

Rate your mood when going to sleep using the emoji score system

Second Step

Tell Sleepmood when you wake up

Rate your mood for the day using the emoji scoring system

Third Step

Rate your overall mood for the day using the emoji score system

Your Data

Sleep history will be displayed on a graph and updated every time you input a sleep entry.


After one month, we will suggest recommended hours of sleep based off your highest scoring mood.
